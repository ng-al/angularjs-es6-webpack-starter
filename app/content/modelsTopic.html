Models

A model with ECMAScript 6 is represented as a class. Without ES6 (and webpack), when you define a model you are forced to either put it into the global space, or turn it into an Angular service recipe so that it can be injected.

Note that in this starter app, models are defined in separate files, and used via an import. See the /app/main/routingItem.model as an example. (It is referenced by /app/main/routing.provider.es6)
